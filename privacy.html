Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Superbuyer Inc. ("Superbuyer," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit superbuyerai.com or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us and basic technical data needed to run the site:

How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide the information or services you request.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate the site and communicate with you (for example, our website host and email provider), and only as needed to perform those services, or where required by law.

Data retention

We keep the information you send us for as long as needed to respond to you and for our legitimate business records, after which we delete or anonymize it.

Your choices

You may request that we access, correct, or delete the personal information you have provided by emailing us at admin@superbuyerai.com. You can also opt out of further communications at any time.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
